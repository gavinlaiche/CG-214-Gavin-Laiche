My name is Gavin Laiche and I am in my second year
at FIT. I am from New Orleans, LA and was
born and raised there. The South is definitely a
whole different ball park than New York City, but
I think I am adjusting very well! One of
the things about the things I miss most about home
is the food. The food in New York City
truly does not compare to the food in New Orleans.

school, but changed my mind and wanted to do fashion at
the last minute. So, I applied to FIT on a whim and got
accepted with early admittance! I chose the business side
of the fashion industry to focus on, but I also wanted
to incorporate my artistic side as well. That is when I discovered the
Creative Technology Minor and knew it would be a perfect fit.
